Resources - Swing Jazz & Blues

"A few of the resources we recommend for discovering music that moves & inspires Swing Jazz/Blues movement."

Jazz Online

"Classic Jazz Online." This website offers samples & MP3 downloads of jazz music recorded in the first half of the 20th century. Artists range from jazz giants like Basie and Ellington to more obscure musicians. And best of all...it's free!

Swing DJs

The most well-known, online Swing DJ Forum. Sign up as a member and discuss various topics with other novice to seasoned DJs and network with DJs worldwide. Feel free to also browse the forum as a guest and peek at articles that interest you.

Spotify

The digital music player that grants you access to a vast library of songs, artists and albums of all genres. Spotify serves great for finding Swing music, building your own DJ playlists, or following other DJs' playlists. Not to mention that it's free and it hooks up to your Facebook account to log in!
